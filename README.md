# 📊 E-commerce Revenue & Customer Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze customer behavior, revenue trends, payment methods, and product performance using a real-world e-commerce dataset.

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project demonstrates an end-to-end Business Intelligence workflow, including data modeling, DAX calculations, KPI design, and interactive dashboard development.

The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor business performance and identify opportunities through intuitive visualizations and filters.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- Power BI
- DAX
- SQL
- Excel
- Data Modeling

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### Executive Overview
- Revenue
- Profit
- Customers
- Orders
- Average Order Value
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Revenue by State
- Payment Method Distribution
- Top Customers
- Interactive Filters

### Product Analytics
- Revenue per Customer
- Revenue per Order
- Average Freight Cost
- Product Categories
- Revenue by Product Category
- Revenue Contribution by Category
- Category Performance Summary Table

---

## 📌 Business Insights

- Credit card payments account for the largest share of transactions.
- Revenue is concentrated in a few high-performing customer states.
- Sales exhibit noticeable peaks across different months, indicating seasonal demand.
- Product categories such as Health & Beauty and Computers & Accessories generate substantial revenue.
- A relatively small segment of customers contributes a significant portion of total sales.
